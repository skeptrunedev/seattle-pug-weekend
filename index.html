0/3 sorted

🔥 Golden Gardens fire pits Sun PM

Free beach fire pits in Ballard — first-come, unlocked 5:30p, fires out by 10p. Bring your own clean, dry firewood (gathering driftwood isn't allowed) + a lighter.

Parking: free on-site lots (lower by the beach, upper by the dog park) but they fill on summer evenings — get there right at the 5:30p unlock to grab a spot + pit and start the fire early. Gates lock at 10:30p.

Open Golden Gardens in Maps ↗

🪵 Firewood + easy firestarter

One stop on the way: Fred Meyer Ballard has firewood bundles (~$13), marshmallows, and all the s'mores fixings.

For a one-match, no-fuss fire, grab fatwood or Duraflame fire starters — light those under the wood and you're going in minutes.

Open Fred Meyer in Maps ↗

🍪 Cookie-dough s'mores

Chocolate-chip cookies (or edible cookie dough) + toasted marshmallow + chocolate square.
